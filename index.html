Recipe #1 - Beef Stew

Ingredients Quantity
beef stew meat 1
carrots 6
celery 6
beef stew broth 1
onion 1

Step 1: Cut the carrots, celery, and onion into small pieces.
Step 2:Add the carrots, celery, onion, beef stew meat, and beef stew broth to the crock pot.
Step 3:Put the lid on the crock pot and set it on low. Cook for 6 hours.
Step 4:Ready to eat!

Recipe #2 - PBJ Sandwich

Ingredients Quantity
slice of white bread 2
peanut butter 1 heaping tablespoon
jelly 1 heaping tablespoon

Step 1: Put both slices of white bread on your plate.
Step 2:Scoop out the peanut butter with a spoon and use a bread knife to spread it on one slice.
Step 3:Scoop out the jelly with a spoon and use a bread knife to spread it on the remaining slice.
Step 4:Put the slices of bread together.
Step 5:Ready to eat!

Recipe #3 - The Very Best Salad Ever!

Ingredients Quantity
plastic container of fresh greens 1
carrots 2
celery 2
cucumber 1

Step 1: Cut carrots, celery, and cucumber into small pieces.
Step 2:Take fresh greens out of the container and wash throughly in warm water.
Step 3:Combine fresh greens, carrots, celery, and cucumbers in a large bowl.
Step 4:Mix the salad with a large spoon and fork.
Step 5:Ready to eat!
